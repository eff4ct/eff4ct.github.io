Project Overview

I'm collaborating with conflict on the project "Root66" which is our evolving cybersecurity site, it allows you to test your cybersecurity skills with a system of «Capture the flag» (CTF) which consists of solving several tests to earn points. Different categories are available like (Programming, OSINT, Pwn, Web, Forensics), all with different levels of difficulty. A ranking and performance monitoring system is also set up. A database system is present on the site to manage the elements specific to each user.

Website link : https://root66.alwaysdata.net/pages/accueil/

Auto-flag system

The site has an automatic flag system, that is when you find the flag, the points are automatically added to your account, which puts you in the ranking.

Challenges

Two challenges are present on the site, future additions will take place in the coming weeks both on the side of challenges and the general aspect of the site.

"La Source d'Eau"

Difficulty: Easy

This challenge awaits you. It’s up to you to play ! You are stuck under water and you will drown... Will you manage to get out ? Find the password to get out !

"La Fabrique de Cookies"

Difficulty: Easy

This challenge awaits you. It’s up to you ! Find a way around this very secure cookie factory !
